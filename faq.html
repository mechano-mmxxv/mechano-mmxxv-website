FAQ

    We have already uploaded the route map of our college. In case you find it difficult, feel free to call any one of our hospitality coordinators. You will have their assistance in no time.

  • 2. What is the registration process?

    You can go through the respective event section for the registration process.

  • 3.Where to pay the registration fee?

    Participants can pay the registration fee for their respective events at the registration desk in the college.

  • 4. What are the necessary things to bring??

    Your college ID are necessary.

  • 5. Will we have assistance if we arrive at night?

    Yes, definitely. There will be no problem at all. We will take care of you at any time. No need to worry about your security.
